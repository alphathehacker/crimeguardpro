Privacy Policy

At CrimeGuard Pro, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email ID, and badge number.
  • Case data, FIR records, and evidence uploads associated with your department.
  • Usage logs and access timestamps for audit and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate users and manage secure logins.
  • Process and store case and evidence data within authorized departments.
  • Improve platform features, security, and performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and audit requirements.

3. Data Security & Retention

We use advanced encryption and secure servers to store data. Your information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose or as required by law. Regular backups and monitoring are performed to prevent unauthorized access.

4. User Rights & Control

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your data by contacting your department administrator or our support team.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience and analyze system usage. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

CrimeGuard Pro may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated revision date.
