Understanding System Status

Status Indicators

  • Operational: Service is running normally with no issues
  • Degraded: Service is functioning but with reduced performance
  • Outage: Service is unavailable or experiencing critical issues
  • Maintenance: Service is temporarily unavailable for scheduled updates

What to Do During an Outage

  • Check this page for real-time updates on service restoration
  • Avoid repeatedly attempting to log in during an outage
  • Contact support if you have urgent business needs
  • Follow our status updates on social media for announcements

Maintenance Windows

Scheduled maintenance is typically performed during off-peak hours to minimize disruption. You will receive advance notice via email for any planned maintenance that may affect your access.
