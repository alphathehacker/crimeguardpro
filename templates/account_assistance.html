Common Account Issues

Cannot Log In

If you're having trouble logging in, try these steps:

  1. Verify you're using the correct email address associated with your account
  2. Check if Caps Lock is enabled (passwords are case-sensitive)
  3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  4. Try using a different browser or incognito/private mode
  5. If you've forgotten your password, use the Password Recovery tool

Account Registration Problems

Having trouble creating a new account?

  1. Ensure all required fields are filled correctly
  2. Check that your email address is valid and not already registered
  3. Verify your phone number format matches the required pattern
  4. Make sure your password meets the security requirements (min 8 characters, includes letters and numbers)
  5. Check your email spam folder for the verification email
  6. Wait a few minutes and try again if you receive a "too many requests" error

Account Locked or Suspended

Your account may be locked for security reasons:

  • Multiple failed login attempts: Account is temporarily locked for 30 minutes
  • Suspicious activity detected: Contact support immediately
  • Policy violation: Review our terms of service and contact support
  • Unpaid fees (if applicable): Resolve payment issues to restore access

To unlock your account, contact our support team with your account email address.

Email Verification Issues

Not receiving verification emails?

  1. Check your spam or junk mail folder
  2. Verify the email address you used during registration
  3. Add noreply@crimeguardpro.gov to your email whitelist
  4. Wait up to 10 minutes for the email to arrive
  5. Request a new verification email from your account settings
  6. Contact support if emails still don't arrive after 24 hours

Changing Account Information

Need to update your account details?

  • Email address: Update from your profile settings (requires verification)
  • Phone number: Change in account settings, verify with SMS code
  • Password: Use the Password Recovery page or change from profile settings
  • Personal information: Edit from your dashboard profile section

Note: Some changes may require additional verification for security purposes.

Account Security Best Practices

Use a strong password: Minimum 8 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of security to your account
Never share your credentials: CrimeGuard Pro staff will never ask for your password
Log out from shared devices: Always log out when using public or shared computers
Review account activity regularly: Check your login history for any suspicious activity
