Travel Safety

  • Share your travel route with a trusted contact.
  • Use verified transportation services or apps.
  • Avoid isolated areas after dark.

Home Security

  • Keep doors and windows locked at all times.
  • Install motion sensor lights near entrances.
  • Do not share personal details with strangers.

Online Safety

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts.
  • Do not share OTPs or confidential info online.
  • Be cautious of phishing emails or fake job offers.

Emergency Readiness

  • Keep emergency numbers saved on your phone.
  • Always carry a charged mobile and ID card.
  • Prepare a small emergency kit at home and car.
